Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 7, 2026  ·  Applies to: All FireChars Products

This Privacy Policy describes how FireChars ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you provide when using our products and services — including the Billing Manager Android app (com.firechars.billingmanager), the Personal Finance OS, and the firechars.in website. By using any FireChars product, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.
01

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you voluntarily submit it — for example, via our contact form or product enquiry.
  • For the Billing Manager app, any customer names, business details, or invoice data you enter is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Information

  • We may automatically collect certain information about your visit to firechars.in such as browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior for statistical and improvement purposes.
  • The Billing Manager app does not collect any analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers automatically.

Cookies

  • Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though certain features may not function properly.
  • Mobile apps published by FireChars do not use cookies.
02

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and contact form submissions.
  • To provide product updates, newsletters, or announcements if you opt in.
  • To improve our website content, app functionality, and user experience.
  • Automatically collected information is used solely for analytics and performance improvement — never sold to third parties.
03

Billing Manager App — Data Practices

The Billing Manager app is designed as a local-first, offline-first tool. All invoice data, customer records, and business information you enter remains on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any app data on remote servers.

  • Storage permissions — used only to save and share PDF invoices from your device.
  • No internet permission — the core app functionality works fully offline.
  • No account required — no login, no profile, no cloud sync.
  • No advertising SDKs — the app contains zero ads and zero ad tracking.
  • No analytics SDKs — we do not embed Firebase, Crashlytics, or similar tools.
04

Third-Party Services

  • Our website may use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. Google Analytics has its own privacy policy available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Our contact form is protected by reCAPTCHA (Google). By submitting the form, you agree to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • The Billing Manager app does not integrate with any third-party services.
05

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. For local app data, security is governed by your device's own security model (screen lock, encryption at rest).

We recommend keeping your device software up to date and using a screen lock to protect your billing data.

06

Your Rights & Choices

  • Opt-out — You can unsubscribe from any communications from us at any time.
  • Access & Correction — You may request access to or correction of your personal information we hold.
  • Deletion — You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
  • App data — Since Billing Manager stores data locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time via Android Settings → Apps → Billing Manager → Clear Data.
07

Children's Privacy

Our products are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

08

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective upon posting to this page. Your continued use of our products after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of those changes. We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
